About Us

CICSoft provides a collaborative environment for members to enhance their technical capabilities while developing applications. CICSoft creates an atmosphere for students to learn application development from the ground up through weekly sessions. Our Lab division aims to develop and publish next-generation apps for UMass and the greater community. Join CICSoft's Annual Appathon to turn your concept into a reality.

Lab Division

Our mission is to create amazing apps for UMass and the surrounding community, in the spirit of Manning CICS' goal of "computing for the common good". Each year, lab developers will be hired to work on certain projects throughout the year. We expect lab developers to have some experience, which they would gain as a member!

Interested in joining our Lab Division?
Reach out to us by email at cicsoft@cs.umass.edu or on our Instagram @cicsoft to indicate your interest in becoming a lab developer!
